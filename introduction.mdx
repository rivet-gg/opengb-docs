## What is Open Game Backend?

- **First-class scripting, designed like a game engine** Easily extend & modify to grow with your backend on top of the [OpenGB Engine](/engine/introduction) using TypeScript; no backend experience required.
- **Modular** Mix, match, & modify modules as needed to fit your game's unique requirements.
- **Secure, load-tested, & resilient** Built to withstand the chaos that games need to grow & stay online. Load testing, rate limits, captchas, strict schemas, <Tooltip tip="...documentation, secrets management, auto-scaling, and backups">and more</Tooltip> are all enforced by default.
- **Batteries included** Provides thoroughly reviewed, tested, and documented modules to get you started quickly & allow you to customize to fit your needs.
- **Open-source & permissively licensed** Apache 2.0 license allows you to adapt, modify, & redistribute freely. _[Trust no one, own your backend.](https://delistedgames.com/gamesparks-service-to-end-in-september-potential-threat-to-thousands-of-titles/)_

See our [quickstart guide](/concepts/quickstart) to get up and running today. See how Open Game Backend compares to other game backends [here](/comparison/playfab).
